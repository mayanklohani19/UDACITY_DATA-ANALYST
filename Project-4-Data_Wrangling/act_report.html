Data Analysis & Visualization¶

I downloaded the Twitter data for the WeRateDogs website. The final dataset contained 2046 tweets. My first major focus went on with the retweet and favorites count. The distribution below shows with the actual data and log Transformed data. The reason why I have transformed into a log is to make the distrubution plot to near normal. From the below graphs it clearly shows that only very few tweets have higher Retweets and favorites, and majority of them fall into right tail distribution.
